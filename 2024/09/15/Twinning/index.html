Twinning

1. Twinning Elements

  • $K_{1}$: composition plane (twin plane)
  • $K_{2}$: the second undistorted plane (rotated but undistorted)
  • $S$: the plane of shear perpendicular to $K_{1}$
  • $\eta_{1}$: shear direction
  • $\eta_{2}$: a rotated, but undistorted direction common to $S$ and $K_{2}$

2. Lattice Reproduction Conditions

  1. Type I twin: $K_{1}$ is rational
  1. Type II twin: $\eta_{1}$ and $K_{2}$ are rational, $\eta_{2}$ and $K_{1}$ are not

Tip: rational means passing through sets of points of the Bravais lattice.
